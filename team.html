As a part of capacity building to build-out, scale, manage and sustain this data repository system in the future, we are building a strong support system made up of Cloud Services partners and campus Information Technology (IT) partners from both UT-Rio Grande Valley and UT-Dallas. This team has been working together in planning, building the prototype, evaluating various architectures as well as building the needed infrastructure for security.
